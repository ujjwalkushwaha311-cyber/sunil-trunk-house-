About Sunil Trunk House

Premium steel storage solutions with a legacy of quality, durability, and customer trust.

Premium Strength. Trusted Craftsmanship.

Sunil Trunk House is a leading manufacturer of steel storage products, specializing in almirahs, trunks, coolers, and stands. Since our founding, we have focused on delivering robust, secure, and stylish solutions for homes and businesses.

We combine precision engineering with high-grade steel and advanced finish techniques to ensure every product is built for long-lasting performance. Our direct-to-customer approach removes intermediaries, allowing us to offer superior quality at competitive prices.

Driven by innovation and customer satisfaction, we are committed to building products that stand the test of time and strengthen your storage experience.
